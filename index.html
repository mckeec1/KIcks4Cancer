WHO IS KIDS KICKING CANCER?


Kids Kicking Cancer is a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization that provides weekly classes for children, both inpatient and outpatient, in the mind-body techniques found in the martial arts. Our mission is to ease the pain of very sick children while empowering them to heal physically, spiritually and emotionally.

WHO WE ARE


We are a growing school that offers classes for all ages, including classes for bully defense and conflict resolution. We promote health, exercise, discipline, patience, perseverance and confidence. We also teach things like self-control, balance and respect as well as humility.
